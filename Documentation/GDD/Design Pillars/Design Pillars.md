# Design Pillars
1. **Spectrum Identity**  
   Every knight is defined by a wavelength: visuals, weapon style, combat role, and effects all derive from light properties.
2. **Solo PvE Mastery**  
   Combat rewards timing, positioning, effect synergy, and enemy learning.
3. **Swap-and-Adapt Gameplay**  
   Unlock multiple knights and **switch** to handle different threats, puzzles, and environments.
4. **Readable, Stylized World**  
   Strong silhouettes, clear VFX, and a consistent **3D chibi armored knight** style keep everything legible and iconic.
