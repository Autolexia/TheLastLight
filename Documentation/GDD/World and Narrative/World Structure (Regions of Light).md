# World Structure (Regions of Light)
Each region reflects a spectrum band in mood, hazards, puzzles, and enemy behavior.

- **Infra Reach (Infrared)**: heat shimmer, forges, thermal ruins; enemies reward DoT pressure.
- **Resonant Foundry (Microwaves)**: humming metallic halls, energy conduits; shielded enemies; interruption focus.
- **Signal Expanse (Radio)**: wide ruins, wave anomalies; swarms; control-focused combat.
- **White Sanctum (Visible / Omni)**: pristine reflective temple-city; projectile-heavy foes; reflect/flash moments.
- **Violet Veil (Ultraviolet)**: shadowed forest/crypts; hidden markings and traps; stealth routes.
- **Boneglass Citadel (X-Ray)**: pale towers, layered walls; armored elites; weak-point mastery.
- **Gamma Scar (Gamma)**: unstable luminous fissures; clustered threats; zoning and AoE pressure.
- **Quantum Rift (Quantum)**: warped geometry, flickering bridges; mobility challenges; teleport/phase gates.
- **Prism Crown (Full Spectrum)**: convergence zone; mixed resistances; mixed final mastery trials.
