# Enemy Families
- **Shadelings**: fast swarms; controlled by Shock/Disorient.
- **Null Knights**: armored elites; countered by Vulnerable and precise hits.
- **Gloomcasters**: ranged attackers; countered by Reflect/Blind.
- **Hollow Sentinels**: slow heavies; countered by Heat/Scorch and zoning.
- **Rift Stalkers**: mobile threats; countered by Teleport/Phase parity and timing.
