# Self-Buffs (Narrative-Compatible)
Self-buffs are framed as the knight “channeling” their wavelength inward.

- **Empower** (Infra): amplify heat output → stronger strikes.
- **Overcharge** (Micro): resonance spike → bigger impact.
- **Haste** (Radio): frequency surge → faster motion/attacks.
- **Regenerate/Cleanse** (Omni): purity of white light → restore/clean.
- **Stealth** (UV): unseen wavelength → lower detection.
- **Amplify** (X-Ray): sharper penetration → enhanced weak-point hits.
- **Fortify** (Gamma): dense energy shielding → boosted defense.
- **Energize** (Quantum): stabilize probability → faster cooldown flow.
- **Shield** (Spectrum): harmonized spectrum → prismatic barrier.
