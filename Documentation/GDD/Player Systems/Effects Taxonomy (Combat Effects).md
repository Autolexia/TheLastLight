# Effects Taxonomy (Combat Effects)
Reusable effects used across kits.

- **Heat**: Damage over time (DoT) that steadily drains enemy health/stamina.
- **Scorch**: Defense reduction over time; increases damage taken.
- **Shock**: Disruption/knockback; interrupts or destabilizes enemies.
- **Disorient**: Slow/confusion; reduces enemy movement or effectiveness.
- **Reflect**: Deflects incoming projectiles/attacks back at enemies.
- **Blind**: Temporarily reduces enemy accuracy/awareness.
- **Vulnerable**: Exposes weak points; increases damage taken.
- **Radiation**: Persistent area damage over time in a lingering field.
- **Teleport**: Instant short reposition for evasion/engage.
- **Phase**: Temporary intangibility/bypass of defenses or obstacles.

> Note: self-debuff mechanics are intentionally excluded for now (future tuning option).
