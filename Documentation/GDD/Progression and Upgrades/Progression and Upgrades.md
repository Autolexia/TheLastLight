# Progression and Upgrades
- **Knight Unlocks**: earned by restoring regions/boss defeats.
- **Skill Trees (per knight)**: enhance signature effects, mobility, control, or survivability.
- **Relics**: passive modifiers that alter effect behavior (e.g., Shock chains, Heat spreads, Reflect strengthens).
- **Mastery Perks**: long-term progression; unlock alternate playstyles for a knight.
