# Audio
Each light band has a sonic identity:
- Infra: low warm rumble
- Micro: buzzing resonance
- Radio: pulsing wave tones
- Omni: clean crystalline chime
- UV: soft hiss / whisper
- X-Ray: thin metallic ring
- Gamma: deep crackling energy
- Quantum: fluttering glitches
- Spectrum: layered harmonic chord
