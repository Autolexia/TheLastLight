# The Last Light

> *"Defend the last light in the world from The Shadow that seeks to consume it."*

🎮 **[Play it on itch.io](https://xariex.itch.io/the-last-light)** — Made for [Pirate Software Game Jam 15](https://itch.io/jam/pirate15)

---

## About

**The Last Light** is an arena-based defense game where you play as a warrior defending a central light source from endless waves of shadow creatures. The game explores the philosophy of Light vs Darkness — as you fight back The Shadow, the light itself grants you new powers, gradually transforming you into a vessel of light.

Built in **72 hours** by Team Reach for Pirate Software Game Jam 15.

---

## Gameplay

You are placed in a circular arena surrounded by The Shadow. Waves of shadow creatures spawn from the darkness at the edges of the map in increasing intensity. Your goal is to keep The Last Light alive.

- Enemies drop light orbs on death that float to the arena's edge — collect them to gain experience, but be careful straying too far from the light.
- The Last Light dims as it gets overwhelmed, and begins taking damage when fully consumed by shadow.
- As you gain experience, the light bestows new abilities upon you.

### Abilities

| Ability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Light Beam** | A heavy beam of light, freely aimed for a short duration |
| **Light Burst** | A radiance burst that knocks back all nearby enemies |
| **Radiance** | Calls on the last light to shine brighter, dispelling all shadows in the arena |

## Theme Interpretation

The jam theme was **Shadows and Alchemy**.

- **Shadows** — The world is overtaken by a physically real entity called *The Shadow*. The player wields light in combat against it.
- **Alchemy** — As the player absorbs more of the light's power, they are transformed — body and soul — into what the world needs: a hero of light.
